OUR PHILOSOPHY

You don't hike to escape society. You hike to rise above it.

ÆTHERPEAK™ is an ultra-luxury alpine performance brand for people who do not technically need expedition-grade gear, but emotionally require it. Designed for environments ranging from Patagonia to the coffee shop.

If other brands are "serious," ÆTHERPEAK is absurdly reverent. We believe that technical excellence is a state of mind, and that true performance transcends the mountain itself.
